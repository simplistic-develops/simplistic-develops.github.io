OnTop — Privacy Policy

Currently, OnTop stores all of your data locally on your device. We do not currently collect, transmit, or share any personal information, and there is no analytics, tracking, or third-party service in the app today.

We may introduce optional features in the future — such as cloud backup or cross-device sync — that store data on our servers. If we do, this policy will be updated first to describe exactly what is stored, how it is used, and how you can opt out, and the app will clearly indicate when any such feature is active or in use.

If you have questions, please contact us at simplistic.develops@gmail.com.
