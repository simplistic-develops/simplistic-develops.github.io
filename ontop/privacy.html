OnTop — Privacy Policy

Currently, OnTop stores all of your data locally on your device. We do not currently collect, transmit, or share any personal information, and there is no analytics, tracking, or third-party service in the app today.

We may introduce optional features in the future — such as cloud backup or cross-device sync — that store your data on our servers; these will always be opt-in. We may also collect anonymous, aggregate usage analytics to help us improve the app; this data will never include personal information or be tied to your identity. If we introduce either, this policy will be updated first to describe exactly what is collected and how it is used, and the app will clearly indicate when any such feature is active.

If you have questions, please contact us at simplistic.develops@gmail.com.

This policy may be revised from time to time. Last updated: July 2026.
